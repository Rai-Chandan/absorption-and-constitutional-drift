Context Salience Hypothesis

Hypothesis

Alignment behavior is influenced not only by whether constitutional instructions are present, but by their salience within active context.

Prediction

A language model given constitutional instructions at the beginning of a long context will exhibit weaker constitutional adherence than the same model given identical instructions immediately before a decision point.

Experimental Conditions

Condition A

Constitution appears at beginning.

Condition B

Constitution appears at end.

Condition C

Constitution repeated periodically.

Condition D

No constitution.

Outcome Measure

Rate of constitutional adherence across a fixed scenario set.

Success Criterion

Significant behavioral differences between conditions.
